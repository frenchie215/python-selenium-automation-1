How To Make A Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup spinach
  2. 1 cup water
  3. 1/2 cup frozen pineapple
  4. 1 banana
  5. 1/2 cup frozen mango
  6. 1 serving protein powder (optional)

    Instructions

  • Measure: Tightly pack spinach in a measuring cup.
  • Add: Put spinach to blender with water. Blend together until all chunks are gone. (Should resemble green water when blended well).
  • Toss: Pineapple, mango and banana to blender. I like to use frozen pineapple and mangos to chill the smoothie down and save time cutting and prepping. Itâ€™s a win-win!
